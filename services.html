Our Course

HLT64121 - Advanced Diploma of Nursing

This course describes the performance results, skills, and knowledge required to register and evaluate a donor's whole blood, red blood cells, platelets, plasma, and other blood components donated as whole blood or through the blood collection process.

HLT54121 - Diploma of Nursing

This course describes the performance outcomes, skills and knowledge required to implement nursing interventions to support the social and emotional health and care needs of older adults.

ACM50219 - Diploma of Veterinary Nursing

This unit of competency describes the skills and knowledge required to prepare for intensive surgery and to support veterinarians during surgery. It is aimed at veterinary nurses who work in veterinary hospitals or emergency/specialist veterinary clinics.

52852WA - Graduate Diploma of Cosmetic Nursing and Injectables

This course describes the performance outcomes, skills and knowledge required to maintain infection prevention during skin penetration therapy and examines the clinic's compliance with applicable state or territory and local council requirements. This unit is intended for individuals making independent treatment decisions.

PREMHA404 - Use mathematics in a nursing and health context

The Pre-Certificate IV in Health and Nursing Studies aims to provide candidates with the basic knowledge and skills to achieve entry requirements for the Diploma in Nursing, Diploma in Anaesthesia Technology, or to support entry to other health or nursing related bachelor's degree levels.

HLTENN048 - Apply nursing practice in the emergency care setting (Release 2)

This course introduces the performance outcomes, skills and knowledge required to work as a member of an urgent care team in an urgent care setting, including hospital emergency departments, primary care services and ambulate hospital services.
