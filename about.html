About the Australian Health University

Established in 1848, the Australian Health University is a full-time undergraduate college in Australia. The motto of the university is:"Medical ethics first, excellence; Studious practice, have long been a good doctor". The Australian Health University focuses on courses and expertise in healthcare and nursing. It has produced many excellent graduates.
